StreetheArt Creators

Three young entrepreneurs seeking to improve our community.



This endeavour was born at the WatchMeCode summer youth enrichment computer science program at Howard University. We were tasked with coming up with business ideas that could benefit the community. Graffiti was the first thing that popped into our heads because it is present throughout America but specifically prominent in D.C. More over;Graffiti is an expression of spirit and culture. The problem that graffiti is facing is that it is associated with vandalism and crime, and now graffiti is starting to fade away in many urban environments. We setout to solve this problem by creating a platform where people can customize their clothes with graffiti art from local and non-local artists, who receive compensation for their art.



STREETHEART was created to connect your Heart to Art. StreetheArt is moving graffiti from the Streets to the Net. We are working to re-brand graffiti form being associated with vandalism and crime to a respectable art form while keeping you looking stylish!!! StreetheArtists can post their work on our website and you can customize and design your very own t-shirts, hoodies, hats, etc, with the graffiti design of your choice at an affordable price. The StreetheArtists will receive all of the profit when their graffiti art is used. We are keeping graffiti art relevant to our times. Post your art to our website, Customize your clothes with the design that you like. Connect your Heart to the Art with StreetheArt.
